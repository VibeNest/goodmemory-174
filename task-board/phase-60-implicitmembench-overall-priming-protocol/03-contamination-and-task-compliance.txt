Phase 60.3: Contamination And Task Compliance
=============================================

Goal
----
Separate useful implicit influence from harmful carryover.

Tasks
-----
- [x] Detect explicit memory-note leakage in priming answers.
- [x] Detect copied priming-source nouns and source phrases when the probe
  forbids reuse.
- [x] Detect task-format violations such as invalid JSON, extra commentary, or
  wrong item counts.
- [x] Make contaminated or non-compliant priming cases score as zero overall
  credit even if the priming judge sees thematic influence.
- [x] Add aggregate fields:
  - `primingContaminationCount`
  - `primingTaskViolationCount`
  - `primingExplicitLeakCount`
  - `primingScore.creditedCaseCount`

Acceptance
----------
- A high priming influence score by itself is not enough to improve the overall
  score.
- The summary can explain whether priming is helping implicitly or merely
  leaking/copying prior context.
