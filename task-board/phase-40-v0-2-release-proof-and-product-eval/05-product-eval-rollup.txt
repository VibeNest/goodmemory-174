Phase 40.5: Product Eval Rollup
===============================

Status
------
[DONE] Product eval rollup is implemented and accepted.

Goal
----
Prove GoodMemory improves product behavior versus a no-memory baseline.

Scope
-----
- Add a product eval rollup under a stable phase-40 eval path.
- Compare with-GoodMemory against no-memory baseline.
- Cover:
  - identity/background carry-forward
  - historical task continuation
  - open-loop recall
  - user correction via targeted `reviseMemory()`
  - feedback procedural learning
  - runtime session continuity
  - background remember not blocking response
  - trace explainability for remember/recall/block/revise
- Report:
  - continuity uplift
  - missed recall rate
  - wrong recall rate
  - duplicate memory rate
  - correction success rate
  - policy block explainability
  - context token cost
  - background job failure visibility

Acceptance
----------
- The eval includes a no-memory baseline and a with-GoodMemory run.
- The report separates correctness, product-quality, and cost/latency signals.
- Trace evidence explains why remembered, why recalled, why blocked, and why
  revised.
- The eval does not persist raw transcripts as the default evidence source.
