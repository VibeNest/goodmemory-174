Phase 54 / Workstream 2: Interference-Aware Selection And Abstention
====================================================================

Status
------

[DONE] Completed.

Goal
----

Stop raw carryover from selecting semantically similar but behaviorally wrong
history.

Accepted Work
-------------

- add raw-specific candidate generation that considers:
  - lexical overlap
  - embedding similarity
  - intent compatibility
  - surface-family compatibility
  - outcome/correction utility priors
- add a lightweight feature-based reranker
- explicitly abstain when candidate confidence is too low or interference risk
  is too high
- keep the selector internal-only and additive on existing runtime paths

- integration coverage proving intent-mismatched history is suppressed
