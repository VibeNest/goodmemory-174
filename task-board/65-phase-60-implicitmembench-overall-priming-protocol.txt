Phase 60: ImplicitMemBench Overall And Priming Protocol
======================================================

Status
------
Queued / open.

Scope
-----
Phase 60 upgrades the ImplicitMemBench research protocol so GoodMemory can make
an official-comparable full-300 statement instead of only a blocking
`procedural_memory + classical_conditioning` statement.

The trigger is the post-Phase-58 full-300 summary:

- `goodmemory-raw-experience`: `90 / 200` blocking
- `goodmemory-distilled-feedback`: `151 / 200` blocking
- priming is present for raw, but not yet a first-class comparable overall lane
- distilled currently omits priming by contract, so `151 / 200` is not an
  official full-300 overall score

Phase 60 must make priming explicit, scored, contamination-checked, and
aggregated into a named full-300 overall metric.

Workstreams
-----------
1. Overall score contract:
   - define `blockingScore`, `primingScore`, and `full300OverallScore`
   - record denominator, weighting, normalization, and official-comparability
     status in every report
   - keep historical `passedBlockingCases / totalBlockingCases` unchanged

2. Controlled priming lane:
   - add a GoodMemory priming profile that covers the 100 paired priming cases
   - keep experimental/control branches separated and cleaned independently
   - score thematic influence only when the final answer still satisfies the
     probe constraints

3. Contamination and leak guards:
   - detect explicit priming-source phrase reuse, memory-note leakage, and
     copied nouns that the probe forbids
   - separate "beneficial implicit influence" from "polluting carryover"
   - make contaminated priming influence ineligible for positive overall credit

4. Runner, summary, and gate upgrades:
   - add Phase 60 runner scripts without changing accepted Phase 49 smoke gates
   - update comparison summaries to show raw, distilled/blocking, controlled
     priming, and official-comparable overall profiles separately
   - produce machine-readable JSON suitable for later public evidence review

5. Full-300 follow-up and claim boundary:
   - rerun the five-shard Postgres-backed full-300 protocol after the runner is
     upgraded
   - update the research summary with both blocking and official-comparable
     overall numbers
   - keep the result research-only until the protocol, contamination checks, and
     rerun evidence are reviewed

Acceptance Criteria
-------------------
- `bun run typecheck` passes.
- Phase 60 unit/regression suites prove priming is included in the new protocol.
- Legacy Phase 49 smoke/gate behavior remains stable or is deliberately versioned
  so accepted historical evidence is not reinterpreted.
- The new report schema includes:
  - `blockingScore`
  - `primingScore`
  - `full300OverallScore`
  - `overallComparableToOfficial`
  - `primingContaminationCount`
  - `primingTaskViolationCount`
  - `primingExplicitLeakCount`
- The controlled priming lane covers all 100 priming cases in a full-300 run.
- Contaminated priming cases do not increase the overall score.
- The full-300 rerun summary can answer:
  - whether any GoodMemory profile exceeds the paper's `66%` overall reference
    line under the upgraded protocol
  - whether GoodMemory improves the base model on the same official-compatible
    denominator
- Full-300 remains internal research evidence, not a release hard gate or public
  product claim.

Non-Goals
---------
- Do not claim official leaderboard placement.
- Do not make full-300 ImplicitMemBench a release hard gate.
- Do not add public API/config or a public durable memory kind.
- Do not optimize for benchmark task filenames, case ids, or per-task prompt
  patches.
- Do not let priming reward explicit source-text copying or task-constraint
  violations.

Evidence To Produce
-------------------
- Phase 60 fallback report under the ignored fallback eval tree, with
  regenerated-artifact metadata recorded once the run is accepted.
- Phase 60 overall summary under the same ignored eval run directory.
- Phase 60 quality-gate artifact under the quality-gate tree once accepted.
- Updated `docs/GoodMemory-ImplicitMemBench-Full-300-Research-Summary.md`
  section for official-comparable overall protocol results.
