Phase 55 / Workstream 2: Candidate Generation, Reranker, And Interference Ledger
=================================================================================

Status
------

[DONE] Completed.

Goal
----

Improve raw candidate selection quality by using multiple retrieval views,
calibrated ranking, and explicit interference accounting.

Accepted Work
-------------

- add multi-view candidate generation over:
  - exact-slot overlap
  - lexical overlap
  - embedding similarity
  - correction/success priors
- add a lightweight internal reranker/training helper
- add an internal interference ledger for:
  - semantically similar but behaviorally wrong carryover
  - failed previously selected exemplars
  - correction-backed safer alternative conflicts
- add abstention when top confidence is too low or the top-2 gap is too small

Verification
------------

- unit coverage for feature vectors, interference penalties, and abstention
  thresholds
- research diagnostics that distinguish `memory_miss`, `wrong_exemplar`,
  `abstain`, and `reasoning_after_correct_retrieval`
