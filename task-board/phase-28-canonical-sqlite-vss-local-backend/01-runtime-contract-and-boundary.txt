Why this exists
---------------
- Phase 26 closed the durable local baseline, but it intentionally stopped short of claiming `sqlite-vss` as the canonical accelerated backend.
- Phase 28 needs one authoritative contract for when local SQLite is truly accelerated versus merely durable.
- The runtime must not silently blur “accelerated”, “fallback”, and “rules-only”.

- unsupported accelerated mode surfaces clear diagnostics
- missing `GOODMEMORY_EMBEDDING_*` still resolves to `rules-only`
