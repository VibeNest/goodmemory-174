Phase 48.2: Threat Model And Privacy Boundary
=============================================

Status
------

[DONE] Hosted/shared privacy boundary defined and enforced by the no-go gate.

Tasks:

[DONE] P48.2-T001 Define auth, tenancy, redaction, export, deletion, and audit
  requirements for any hosted/shared scope.
[DONE] P48.2-T002 Define raw-transcript and assistant-output persistence
  boundaries.
[DONE] P48.2-T003 Block hosted claims when privacy or deletion semantics are
  incomplete.

Accepted Boundary:

- Hosted/shared scope must define auth, tenancy, redaction, export, deletion,
  and audit semantics before implementation.
- Raw transcript persistence is blocked by default.
- A hosted claim remains blocked when deletion/export/audit semantics are
  incomplete.
- Phase 48 implements no hosted storage, account, analytics, sync, or team
  workspace runtime.
