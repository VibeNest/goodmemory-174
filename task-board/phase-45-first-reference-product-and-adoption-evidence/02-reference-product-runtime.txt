Acceptance Criteria
-------------------

- A developer can boot the product from documented commands.
- The product works in rules-only mode with no embedding or assisted extraction
  provider env.
- Provider-backed retrieval can be enabled optionally without changing the
  product contract.
- Runtime archive remains off unless explicitly enabled for a bounded test.
