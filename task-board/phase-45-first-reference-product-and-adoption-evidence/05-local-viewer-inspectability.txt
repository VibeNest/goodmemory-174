Acceptance Criteria
-------------------

- The viewer remains optional, local-only, token-gated, and read-only.
- Viewer output is useful for debugging product memory behavior.
- Forget/revise handoff commands are generated for operator review, not executed
  by the browser.
- Reference-product backend forget/revise may use authenticated HTTP bridge
  mutation endpoints; the viewer remains read-only.
- Scope checks continue to reject cross-scope `gmrec:v1` handoff refs.
